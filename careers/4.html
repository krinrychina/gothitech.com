Graphic Design Intern

Office / Remote (as per company policy) Internship

Job Overview

We are looking for a creative and motivated Graphic Design Intern to join our design team.
This internship is ideal for students or fresh graduates who want hands-on industry experience in visual design, social media creatives, and basic motion content while working under the guidance of experienced designers.

The intern will gain practical exposure to real client and company projects while developing strong fundamentals in graphic design, branding, and digital media.

Key Responsibilities

The intern will assist the design team in the following areas:

  • Support the creation of visual content for social media, websites, and digital campaigns

  • Assist in designing:

    • Social media post images

    • Banners, blog images, and info graphics

    • Website banners and promotional creatives

  • Help with basic image editing, background removal, and layout adjustments

  • Assist in preparing creatives for web and print formats

  • Support basic motion graphics or video content (if interested)

  • Follow brand guidelines and design instructions provided by senior designers

  • Collaborate with marketing and content teams under supervision

  • Learn to manage multiple design tasks with deadlines in a professional environment

Learning Exposure

  • Real-world design workflow for web & social media

  • Brand consistency and visual storytelling

  • Basics of motion graphics and video content

  • Understanding design requirements for digital platforms

  • Working with feedback and revisions professionally

Requirements

  • Basic understanding of Graphic Design fundamentals

  • Familiarity with at least one of the following tools:

    • Adobe Photoshop

    • Adobe Illustrator

  • Understanding of:

    • Color theory

    • Typography

    • Layout and visual hierarchy

  • Creative mindset with attention to detail

  • Willingness to learn, take feedback, and improve

  • Ability to work independently and as part of a team

Education

  • No strict degree requirement

  • Students, fresh graduates, or self-learners are welcome

  • Training or certification in Graphics / UI design is a plus

  • Portfolio or sample work is highly encouraged

Preferred / Plus Skills

  • Basic knowledge of:

    • Motion graphics

    • Video editing tools

  • Interest or basic exposure to:

    • UI/UX design

    • Social media design trends

  • Familiarity with AI-assisted design tools

  • Basic understanding of digital marketing visuals

  • Basic HTML/CSS knowledge

Ideal Candidate Profile

  • Creative, proactive, and detail-oriented

  • Passionate about design and visual storytelling

  • Open to learning and professional growth

  • Responsible and sincere about assigned tasks

Career Growth Opportunity

  • Hands-on industry experience

  • Mentorship from senior designers

  • Opportunity to build a strong professional portfolio

  • Potential future consideration for junior/full-time roles based on performance

Compensation & Benefits

  • Internship allowance (as per company policy)

  • Performance-based growth opportunity

  • Certificate of completion (if applicable)

  • Salary review upon long-term engagement
